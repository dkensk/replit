## What Happens Now

### Step 1: Processing (Current Step) ‚è≥
- **Time:** Usually 10-30 minutes, sometimes up to a few hours
- **What Apple does:**
  - Validates your app
  - Processes the binary
  - Checks for compliance issues
  - Generates app previews (if needed)

### Step 2: Ready to Submit ‚úÖ
- Once processing completes, your app will show **"Ready to Submit"**
- You'll see it in App Store Connect under your app's **"App Store"** tab
- The build will appear in the **"Build"** section

### Step 3: Complete App Information üìù
Before submitting, you need to fill in:

1. **App Information:**
   - App description
   - Keywords
   - Support URL
   - Marketing URL (optional)
   - Privacy Policy URL (if your app collects data)

2. **Pricing and Availability:**
   - Price (or Free)
   - Availability (which countries)

3. **App Privacy:**
   - Privacy policy (required if you collect data)
   - Data collection details

4. **Version Information:**
   - What's New in This Version
   - App Category
   - Age Rating
   - License Agreement

5. **App Store Listing:**
   - **Screenshots** (REQUIRED):
     - iPhone 6.7" Display (iPhone 14 Pro Max, etc.)
     - iPhone 6.5" Display (iPhone 11 Pro Max, etc.)
     - iPhone 5.5" Display (iPhone 8 Plus, etc.)
     - iPad Pro (12.9-inch) - if your app supports iPad
   - **App Icon** (1024x1024 pixels)
   - App Preview videos (optional)

### Step 4: Submit for Review üöÄ
- Once all information is complete, click **"Submit for Review"**
- Apple will review your app (typically 1-7 days)
- You'll get email notifications about the status

### Step 5: In Review üîç
- Apple reviews your app
- They may request changes or ask questions
- You'll be notified via email

### Step 6: Approved! üéä
- Your app goes live on the App Store!
- You'll receive an email confirmation
- You can choose when to release it (immediately or schedule)
