## How to Actually Run It

### Step 1: Look for the Button

1. **You're on the Actions tab** - Good!
2. **You clicked "Build iOS App"** - Good!
3. **Now look at the TOP RIGHT of the page**

### Step 2: Find "Run workflow" Button

The button is usually:
- **At the very top right** of the page
- **Above the "No runs yet" message**
- **Next to search/filter boxes**
- **Blue button** that says "Run workflow"

### Step 3: Click It Properly

1. **Click the blue "Run workflow" button** (top right)
2. **A dropdown/popup will appear** with:
   - Branch selector (should show "main")
   - A green "Run workflow" button
3. **Click the green "Run workflow" button** in the dropdown
4. **Wait 10 seconds, then refresh the page**

## If You Still Don't See It

1. **Scroll to the very top** of the page
2. **Look at the top right corner** (not the sidebar)
3. **It might be next to "Filter" or search icon**
4. **Try a different browser** if needed

## After Clicking

1. **Wait 10-20 seconds**
2. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Cmd+R)
3. **You should see a new entry** showing "Queued" or "In progress"
